FAQ

Questions About Our Services

Everything you need to know before booking.

What services do you offer?

We offer wedding photography, cinematic videography, pre-wedding shoots, birthday photography, corporate event coverage, and general event photography across India.

Do you travel for destination shoots?

Absolutely. We cover destination weddings and shoots across India. Travel and accommodation are added to your quote based on location.

How far in advance should I book?

For weddings, 2–3 months ahead is ideal. For birthdays and smaller events, 2–3 weeks is usually sufficient.

Can I customise a package?

Yes! Every event is different. We're happy to build a custom plan with the exact hours, team size, and deliverables you need.

What is your editing style?

Our style is timeless and emotive — warm tones, true-to-life colours, and subtle enhancements that make images feel like art, not filters.
