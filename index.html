FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the things couples and clients ask us most before booking.

✦
How far in advance should we book?

We recommend booking 2–3 months ahead for weddings and major events to lock in your preferred date and team. For birthdays and smaller shoots, 1–2 weeks is usually enough.

How many photos will we receive?

It depends on the package and duration of the event — anywhere from 200 to 1000+ professionally edited, high-resolution images delivered through a private online gallery.

Do you travel outside Mumbai for shoots?

Yes! We cover destination weddings and shoots across India. Travel and accommodation are added to your quote based on the location.

How long does it take to receive our final gallery?

Standard delivery is 7–10 days depending on the package. Highlight films and albums may take a little longer as they're crafted with extra care.

Can we customise a package?

Absolutely. Every event is different, so we're happy to mix and match coverage hours, add a second photographer, drone shots, or albums to build a plan that fits your day and budget.
