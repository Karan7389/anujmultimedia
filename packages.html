FAQ

Pricing Questions, Answered

Everything you need to know before choosing your package.

Can I customise a package?

Absolutely. We can add hours, extra photographers, drone footage, or an album to any base package. Just get in touch and we'll build a plan that fits your day perfectly.

Are prices negotiable for budget weddings?

We believe everyone deserves beautiful wedding photos. Tell us your budget and we'll try our best to create something that works for you.

What is included in the edited photos?

All photos are professionally colour-graded, retouched, and delivered in full resolution — ready for printing, sharing, and framing.

How do I receive my photos?

All edited photos are delivered via a private, password-protected online gallery that you can share with family and friends and download at any time.

Is a booking deposit required?

Yes, we require a 30% deposit to hold your date. The remaining balance is due 7 days before the event. We accept all major payment methods.
